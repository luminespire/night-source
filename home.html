Night

A thumb alpha keyboard layout.

Design

Night is layout designed for typing efficiency with specific targets for comfort.

SFB & SFS

Night features extremely low same-finger and skip same-finger usageEx. MY | MAY.

These enable Night to be efficient at high typing speeds.

Alternation

Night is an alternation heavy layout.

This makes typing feel consistent and rhythmic; albeit not for everyoneEx. TIME.

Redirects

Night focuses heavily on minimizing redirects, particularly bad redirects.

This reduces awkward sequencesEx. SAD.

Effort Map

Night specifically places high usage letters on strong and dexterit fingers.

This helps balance typing load and take advantage of the characteristics of each finger.
